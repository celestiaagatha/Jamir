Sinigang (Filipino Sour Soup)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb pork belly or ribs, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 6–8 okra
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 daikon radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong) or bok choy
  • Long beans (sitaw), cut into 2–3 inch lengths
  • Green chilies (siling haba), optional
  • Tamarind pulp or sinigang mix, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine pork and water. Bring to a boil and skim off any scum.
  2. Add tomatoes, onion, and fish sauce. Lower heat and simmer until pork is tender.
  3. Add radish, eggplant, okra, and long beans. Cook until tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in tamarind pulp or sinigang mix to reach your preferred sourness.
  5. Add leafy greens and chilies. Simmer 1–2 minutes more.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with rice.
